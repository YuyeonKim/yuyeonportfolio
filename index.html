About Me

Multimedia Designer | UX Designer, Media Marketer

I’m Yuyeon Jessica Kim, a Bachelor of Digital Media student at RMIT University specialising in UX design, multimedia, and digital marketing.

My work focuses on user centred design, crafting interaction concepts that prioritise clarity, usability, and engagement. Through my projects, I explore user journeys, test ideas, and refine outcomes based on feedback. Alongside this, I have experience in video editing, visual design, and content creation, and I am confident adapting to new tools and workflows.

My background in hospitality and retail has strengthened my communication skills, adaptability, and ability to respond to user needs in fast paced settings.

I am passionate about combining design, technology, and storytelling, particularly within multimedia and immersive experiences, and I am always looking for opportunities to grow through real world projects and collaborative environments.
